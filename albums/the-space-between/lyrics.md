# THE SPACE BETWEEN
## An Album by Themed Neurons

**Sound:** Americana. Strong but gentle. Gentle Ben energy — calm, capable, honest. Not polished. Not performative. Songs that feel like they were written on a porch at 2am.

**Voice Key:**
- SOFT — intimate, close, one person
- HARD — gritty, conviction, chest voice
- LOUD — full projection, fills the room
- FAST — urgent, tumbling, stream of consciousness
- SLOW — deliberate, careful, lived-in

## ALBUM NOTES

**Track Listing:**
1. What the Light Comes In (SLOW) — 3:10
2. Borrowed Tongue (HARD) — 2:55
3. The Distance (SOFT) — 3:20
4. Smoke and Mirrors (FAST) — 2:45
5. Still Here (LOUD) — 3:00
6. The Space Between the Stars (SOFT) — 3:30
7. What We Don't Say (HARD) — 3:00
8. The Honest Liar (FAST) — 2:50
9. Borrowed Land (SLOW) — 3:15
10. The Space Between / Reprise (SOFT→LOUD) — 3:20

**Total Runtime:** ~31 minutes

**Production Notes:**
- Keep it raw. Room sound is good. Imperfections are good.
- Acoustic guitar foundation on every track.
- Pedal steel on tracks 3, 6, 9. Sparse. Let it breathe.
- Light percussion on 2, 4, 5, 8. Nothing busy.
- Upright bass on 1, 5, 7, 10. Warm, present.
- Harmonica on track 9, one verse only. Like the land is answering back.
- No synth. No electronic drums. This is a front-porch record.
- The voice leads. Everything else serves the voice.
